As the name suggests,bussiness-to-bussiness marketing refers to the marketing of products or
services to other bussinesses and organizations.It holds several key distinctions from B2C
marketing,which is oriented toward consumers.


In a broad sence,B2B marketing content tends to be more informational and straightforward than
B2C.This is because bussiness purchase decisions,in comparison to those of consumers,are based
more on bottom-line revenue impact.Return on investment (ROI) is rarely a consideration for the
everyday person at least in a monetary sence but it is a primary focus for corperate decision
makers.
